Step-by-Step Startup Guide for Veterans

  • Pick a problem: What do people struggle with that you can solve?
  • Choose your customer: Who will pay for the solution?
  • Create a simple offer: What you sell, the price, and what’s included.
  • Validate quickly: Talk to 10 people, get feedback, and adjust.
  • Build the basics: Name, logo, website, and a way to get paid.
  • Market consistently: Use social media, networking, and referrals.
  • Track money: Monitor expenses, revenue, profit, and taxes.

Tip: Keep it simple. Small, consistent actions beat a perfect plan you never start.
