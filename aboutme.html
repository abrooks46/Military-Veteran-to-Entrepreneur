About Me

I am a Military Veteran turned Entrepreneur committed to helping other veterans transition successfully into business ownership. I'm a husband, father, and God-fearing man who is family-oriented, ambitious, and committed to helping others succeed.

I support veterans transitioning into entrepreneurship because I believe we already possess the foundation for business success — discipline, leadership, teamwork, and the ability to perform under pressure.

My military experience taught me to stay focused on the mission, lead with integrity, and solve problems when resources are limited. Entrepreneurship requires that same mindset: identify a problem, build a plan, execute, and adapt.

My Mission

My mission is to provide veterans with a clear path toward entrepreneurship by sharing practical steps, supportive programs, and real examples of veterans who turned their service into a successful business career.

Why This Matters

  • Veterans often face a major shift in identity and routine after separation.
  • Many seek renewed purpose, independence, and belonging in the business world.
  • Business ownership can be a powerful path — when supported by the right resources and planning.

My Values

  • Service: Helping others succeed
  • Discipline: Consistent action beats motivation
  • Community: No one wins alone
  • Growth: Learn, improve, and keep moving
